About

“We’ve done this for years. Why change now?” This is something I’ve heard countless times where I’ve had to sell my ideas and advocate for improvements. I am constantly asking, “How can this be done better?”

I’m Lananh, a user experience designer focused on curating thoughtful and effective solutions. Graduating from the University of Michigan with a degree in Chemical Engineering, I began my career at a utility company where I spent over six years working on multi-functional teams, managing large projects, and mentoring junior employees. I often found myself defending users for making errors, shifting the focus from user error to flawed design decisions. Naturally, I pivoted career paths to UX design.

I believe maintaining a work-life balance is the key to achieving a healthy lifestyle. I exercise my mind and body through gardening, rock-climbing, cooking, and continually exposing myself to new experiences.

Currently, I am looking to join a team where I can use my creativity and analytical background to create delightful user experience. I’d love to connect on LinkedIn!
